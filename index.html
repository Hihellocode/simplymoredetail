About Simply More Detail

The 'Simply More'
Experience Awaits

Based in Miramar, FL, Simply More Detail is your trusted mobile and garage-based detailing studio. We specialize in Gtechniq ceramic coatings, exterior detailing, and interior transformations — all with a personal touch you won't find anywhere else.

Owner Basem started from the ground up, and that journey shows in every car he touches. No shortcuts. No upselling products you don't need. Just genuine care, premium products, and results that make you want to go for a joyride.

Why It Matters

Florida's Elements Will
Destroy Your Paint

☀️

UV & Heat Shield

South Florida's relentless sun causes oxidation, fading, and clear coat failure. Ceramic coating blocks UV at the molecular level — preserving gloss for years.

💧

Hydrophobic Beading

Rain, car wash chemicals, and humidity bead off effortlessly. Your paint stays cleaner longer and maintenance becomes dramatically easier.

🛡️

Contaminant Defense

Bird droppings, tree sap, and salt air can't bond to a coated surface. Chemical resistance prevents permanent staining and etching.

💎

Showroom Gloss

The depth and mirror-like clarity ceramic adds to paint is unmatched by any wax or sealant — and it lasts 1 to 5 years, not weeks.
